    People and teams - find out who's who

    There are almost 700 people working in the Student Education Service. This page provides information on the teams and some of the people involved.

    Service Directors

    The service leadership team is made up of Directors of Student Operations, Student Opportunity and Admissions.

    Director of Student Opportunity - Robert Partridge
    Director of Admissions - Vikki Wright

    Faculty Education Service Managers

    Each Faculty has a Faculty Education Service Manager (FESM). FESMs are responsible for leading and managing the provision of student services within their faculty, as well as shaping and influencing University-wide strategies. They do this by working in partnership with central student services and other University communities. FESMs are also the convenors of the Cross-Institutional Functional Management Teams (CIFMTs).

    School Education Service Managers

    These members of staff are based in the school and are involved in supporting learning and teaching activities. They build on existing good practice found in the school and faculty.
